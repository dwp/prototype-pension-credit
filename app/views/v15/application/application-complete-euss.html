What happens next

The EU Settlement Scheme will tell us once (your / the applicant's) status has been awarded. We will then contact (you / them) to check the details of this application.

If (you / they) hear from the EU Settlement Scheme before we contact (you / them) , (you / they) can call the Pension Credit claim line.

Pension Credit claim line

  • Telephone: 0808 100 6165
  • Textphone (Text Relay): 0808 100 2198

Monday to Friday, 10:00am to 4:00pm (closed on bank holidays).
Contacting 08 and 03 numbers.

If your application is successful we will pay your Pension Credit into the same account as your State Pension.

You will be able to change this to a different account once your Pension Credit is in payment.

Other help you could get

There is other help that you could get if you:

  • have a disability
  • care for someone for 35 hours or more a week
  • need help with your living costs such as paying rent or Council Tax
  • need help heating your home

To check what other help you could get, use one of these benefits calculators.
