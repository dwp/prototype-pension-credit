Task list variations

Before you start: This prototype uses URL query parameters to show/hide variations in content and journeys. Clear prototype data and check your URL if something doesn't look right.

Pay (Later Life)

  1. A single claimant, with Workplace & Private Pensions(W&PP)
  2. A claimant with a partner
  3. A claimant with a partner and housing costs

N.B.1 Claimant's with a Partner as well as W&PP will not be allowed through Later Life filters for the initial release (June 2026)

N.B.2 Claimant's with either a Partner or W&PP could have housing cost tasks

Nil (AfPC)

  1. A nil award claim where State Pension alone exceeds Guarantee Credit amount
  2. A nil award claim with Workplace & Private Pensions
The nil award claim journey uses a query parameter of taskType=nil.
